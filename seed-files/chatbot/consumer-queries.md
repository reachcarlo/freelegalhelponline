# Consumer Mode — Sample Queries

Use these with the chat set to **Consumer** mode. Each scenario includes an opening question and follow-ups to demonstrate multi-turn conversation (max 3 turns).

---

## Scenario 1: Basic Rights Question (Simple Answer)

**Query 1:**
> My employer hasn't paid me for the last two weeks. What are my rights?

**Follow-up 2:**
> Can I file a complaint even though I'm undocumented?

**Follow-up 3:**
> How long do I have to file?

**What it demos:** Basic wage theft rights, immigration status protections, deadlines. Shows consumer-friendly language and "Next Steps" format.

---

## Scenario 2: Wrongful Termination (Multi-Issue)

**Query 1:**
> I was fired after telling my boss I was pregnant. Is that legal?

**Follow-up 2:**
> What if they say it was because of my performance?

**Follow-up 3:**
> Should I file with CRD or EEOC?

**What it demos:** FEHA pregnancy discrimination, pretext analysis, agency routing. Shows how the system identifies multiple legal theories from a single fact pattern.

---

## Scenario 3: Meal and Rest Breaks

**Query 1:**
> My company makes us work through lunch and doesn't give us breaks. What can I do?

**Follow-up 2:**
> How much money can I get for missed breaks?

**What it demos:** Meal/rest break premiums (1 hour of pay each), Labor Code sections 226.7 and 512. Shows calculator-like output in consumer mode.

---

## Scenario 4: Hostile Work Environment

**Query 1:**
> My coworker keeps making racist comments and my manager won't do anything about it. What should I do?

**Follow-up 2:**
> I already complained to HR three times. Does that matter?

**Follow-up 3:**
> Can I sue them personally or just the company?

**What it demos:** FEHA harassment, employer liability for failure to prevent, individual vs. entity liability. Shows detailed "What You Should Know" section.

---

## Scenario 5: Retaliation After Complaint

**Query 1:**
> I reported safety violations to Cal/OSHA and now my hours have been cut. Is this retaliation?

**Follow-up 2:**
> What evidence do I need to prove retaliation?

**What it demos:** Whistleblower protections (Labor Code 1102.5), burden of proof, temporal proximity. Good for showing citation-backed answers.

---

## Scenario 6: Independent Contractor Misclassification

**Query 1:**
> I work full-time for one company but they pay me as a 1099 contractor. Am I misclassified?

**Follow-up 2:**
> What benefits am I missing out on as a misclassified worker?

**Follow-up 3:**
> Where do I file a complaint about this?

**What it demos:** ABC test (AB 5), misclassification consequences, EDD vs. DLSE filing. Shows how system explains the legal test in plain language.

---

## Scenario 7: Family Leave Denied

**Query 1:**
> My employer says I can't take time off to care for my sick parent. Don't I have a right to family leave?

**Follow-up 2:**
> I've only been there 10 months. Does that matter?

**What it demos:** CFRA eligibility (12 months / 1,250 hours), small employer exception, kin care. Shows how conditional eligibility is explained clearly.

---

## Scenario 8: Wage Theft — Final Paycheck

**Query 1:**
> I was fired on Monday and still haven't received my last paycheck. It's been a week. What are my rights?

**Follow-up 2:**
> What are waiting time penalties?

**What it demos:** Labor Code 201-203, waiting time penalties (up to 30 days of wages). Great for showing the calculator integration suggestion.

---

## Scenario 9: Edge Case — Vague Question

**Query 1:**
> Can my employer do this to me?

**What it demos:** How the system handles ambiguous queries — should ask clarifying questions rather than guess.

---

## Scenario 10: Edge Case — Out of Scope

**Query 1:**
> How do I file for divorce in California?

**What it demos:** System correctly identifies this as outside employment law scope and redirects. Tests adversarial/boundary handling.
