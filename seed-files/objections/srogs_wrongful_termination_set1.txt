SPECIAL INTERROGATORIES

DEFINITIONS

As used herein, the term "DOCUMENT" or "DOCUMENTS" refers to any writing as defined in Evidence Code Section 250. The term "YOU" or "YOUR" refers to Defendant TechVision Inc., its officers, directors, employees, agents, representatives, subsidiaries, and affiliates. The term "PLAINTIFF" refers to Plaintiff Maria Santos. The term "INCIDENT" refers to the termination of Plaintiff's employment on or about September 12, 2025. The term "COMMUNICATION" includes any oral, written, or electronic exchange of information.

INSTRUCTIONS

In answering these interrogatories, furnish all information available to you, including information in the possession of your attorneys, investigators, employees, agents, and others acting on your behalf. If you cannot answer an interrogatory in full, answer to the extent possible and explain why you cannot answer fully.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 1:
State all facts supporting YOUR decision to terminate PLAINTIFF's employment.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 2:
Identify each and every DOCUMENT, COMMUNICATION, email, text message, instant message, Slack message, Microsoft Teams message, voicemail, handwritten note, memorandum, letter, report, spreadsheet, database entry, calendar invitation, meeting agenda, meeting minutes, and any other record of any kind whatsoever that relates in any way to PLAINTIFF, her employment, her performance, her conduct, her attendance, her interactions with any person, or any other matter concerning PLAINTIFF, from the date of her hire through the present date and continuing.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 3:
State the names, current addresses, current telephone numbers, current email addresses, job titles, and dates of employment of ALL persons who participated in, contributed to, were consulted about, were informed of, witnessed, overheard, or had any knowledge whatsoever of the decision to terminate PLAINTIFF's employment.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 4:
State YOUR annual gross revenue, net revenue, total payroll expenses, executive compensation packages, and profit margins for fiscal years 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 5:
For EACH performance review or evaluation of PLAINTIFF during her employment with YOU, state the date of the review, the name and title of the reviewer, the overall rating given, and whether any areas of concern were identified.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 6:
Describe in detail YOUR progressive discipline policy, including each step in the process, and state whether each step was followed with respect to PLAINTIFF prior to her termination.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 7:
State all facts supporting YOUR contention, if any, that PLAINTIFF's termination was for legitimate, non-discriminatory, and non-retaliatory reasons.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 8:
Identify all employees terminated by YOU during the period January 1, 2023 through the present, and for each such employee state: (a) the employee's name; (b) job title; (c) date of termination; (d) reason for termination; (e) age at time of termination; (f) race and/or ethnicity; (g) gender; (h) whether the employee had made any complaints of discrimination or harassment prior to termination.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 9:
State all facts and identify all DOCUMENTS relating to any complaint, grievance, report, or allegation of discrimination, harassment, or retaliation made by any employee of YOUR company at any time.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 10:
For each attorney-client privileged COMMUNICATION that YOU are withholding in response to PLAINTIFF's discovery requests, identify the date, author, recipient, and general subject matter of each such COMMUNICATION.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 11:
State what investigations, if any, YOU conducted into PLAINTIFF's complaints of discrimination, including the dates of the investigation, the name of the investigator, persons interviewed, DOCUMENTS reviewed, and conclusions reached.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 12:
Identify all expert witnesses YOU intend to call at trial and state the subject matter of their expected testimony.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 13:
State YOUR complete social media policy, internet usage policy, and electronic communications monitoring policy in effect during PLAINTIFF's employment, and identify every instance in which YOU monitored, accessed, or reviewed PLAINTIFF's personal electronic communications, social media accounts, or personal devices.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 14:
State whether PLAINTIFF ever received any written or verbal warnings during her employment and, if so, identify the date, substance, and author of each warning.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 15:
Describe the process by which YOU selected PLAINTIFF for termination as opposed to other employees in her department, including all criteria considered and the weight given to each criterion.
