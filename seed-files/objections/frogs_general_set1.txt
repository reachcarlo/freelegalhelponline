PROPOUNDING PARTY: Plaintiff Angela Park
RESPONDING PARTY: Defendant NovaTech Solutions Inc.
SET NUMBER: 1

FORM INTERROGATORIES — GENERAL

The following Form Interrogatories are propounded pursuant to CCP section 2030.010 et seq. and Judicial Council Form DISC-001. Defendant is requested to answer each interrogatory under oath.

INTERROGATORY NO. 1.1:
State the name, ADDRESS, telephone number, and relationship to you of each PERSON who prepared or assisted in the preparation of the responses to these interrogatories.

INTERROGATORY NO. 2.1:
State the ADDRESS of each PERSON who has knowledge of any relevant facts relating to this action, and the substance of each such PERSON's knowledge.

INTERROGATORY NO. 2.2:
Have YOU OR ANYONE ACTING ON YOUR BEHALF interviewed any individual concerning the INCIDENT? If so, for each interview state: (a) the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number of the individual interviewed; (b) the date of the interview; (c) the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number of the PERSON who conducted the interview; (d) whether a transcript, recording, or written summary of the interview exists.

INTERROGATORY NO. 4.1:
At the time of the INCIDENT, was there in effect any policy of insurance through which you were or might be insured in any manner for the damages, claims, or actions that have arisen out of the INCIDENT? If so, for each policy state: (a) the name of the insurance company; (b) the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number of each named insured; (c) the number of the policy; (d) the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number of the custodian of the policy; (e) whether any reservation of rights or denial of coverage has been issued by the insurance company, and attach a copy.

INTERROGATORY NO. 6.1:
Do you attribute any physical, mental, or emotional injuries to the INCIDENT? If so, identify each injury.

INTERROGATORY NO. 6.2:
Are any of the injuries identified in your response to Interrogatory 6.1 still causing you problems? If so, for each problem state: (a) a description; (b) whether the problem is subsiding, remaining the same, or getting worse; (c) the frequency and duration of each problem.

INTERROGATORY NO. 6.3:
Are you presently receiving any treatment for injuries you attribute to the INCIDENT? If so, for each treatment state: (a) the name and ADDRESS of each HEALTH CARE PROVIDER providing treatment; (b) the condition or complaint for which treatment is being provided; (c) the dates of treatment.

INTERROGATORY NO. 6.4:
Have you received any consultation or treatment from a HEALTH CARE PROVIDER for any condition that you attribute to the INCIDENT? If so, for each HEALTH CARE PROVIDER state: (a) the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number; (b) the type of consultation or examination; (c) the dates of consultation or treatment; (d) the charges to date.

INTERROGATORY NO. 7.1:
Do you attribute any loss of income or earning capacity to the INCIDENT? If so, state: (a) the nature of the loss; (b) the amount of the loss; (c) the computation showing how the amount of loss was calculated; (d) the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number of each PERSON with knowledge of any facts upon which you base your claim.

INTERROGATORY NO. 8.1:
Have you ever had complaints or injuries that involved the same part of your body or the same condition claimed to have been caused by the INCIDENT? If so, for each prior complaint or injury state: (a) a description of the complaint or injury; (b) the dates of the complaint or injury; (c) the name and ADDRESS of each HEALTH CARE PROVIDER who treated you; (d) the nature of the treatment; (e) whether you are fully recovered.

INTERROGATORY NO. 12.1:
State the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number of each individual: (a) who witnessed the INCIDENT or the events occurring immediately before or after the INCIDENT; (b) who made any statement at the scene of the INCIDENT; (c) who heard any statements made about the INCIDENT by any individual at the scene; (d) who YOU OR ANYONE ACTING ON YOUR BEHALF claim has knowledge of the INCIDENT.

INTERROGATORY NO. 15.1:
Identify each denial of a material allegation and each special or affirmative defense in your pleadings and for each: (a) state all facts upon which you base the denial or defense; (b) state the names, ADDRESSES, and telephone numbers of all PERSONS who have knowledge of those facts; (c) identify all DOCUMENTS and other tangible things that support your denial or defense.

INTERROGATORY NO. 17.1:
Is your response to each request for admission served with these interrogatories an unqualified admission? If not, for each response that is not an unqualified admission: (a) state the number of the request; (b) state all facts upon which you base your response; (c) state the names, ADDRESSES, and telephone numbers of all PERSONS who have knowledge of those facts; (d) identify all DOCUMENTS and other tangible things that support your response.
