PROPOUNDING PARTY: Plaintiff Robert Martinez
RESPONDING PARTY: Defendant Golden State Construction Inc.
SET NUMBER: 1

REQUESTS FOR ADMISSION

DEFINITIONS

"YOU" or "YOUR" means Defendant Golden State Construction Inc. "PLAINTIFF" means Robert Martinez. "WORKSITE" means the construction project at 1200 Harbor Boulevard, Long Beach, California.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 1:
Admit that PLAINTIFF was employed by YOU as a Site Foreman from March 2019 through August 2025.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 2:
Admit that on or about July 10, 2025, PLAINTIFF reported unsafe working conditions at the WORKSITE to YOUR Director of Operations, Frank Delgado.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 3:
Admit that the unsafe conditions reported by PLAINTIFF included failure to install required fall protection on scaffolding above six feet, inadequate trench shoring, and failure to provide hard hats to workers.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 4:
Admit that Cal/OSHA cited YOU for serious violations at the WORKSITE on or about July 25, 2025.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 5:
Admit that PLAINTIFF's employment was terminated on August 8, 2025, less than thirty days after he reported safety violations.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 6:
Admit that no other Site Foreman employed by YOU at the WORKSITE was terminated during the period July 1, 2025 through September 30, 2025.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 7:
Admit that PLAINTIFF did not receive any written warnings about job performance in the six months prior to his termination.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 8:
Admit or deny each of the following: (a) that YOU were aware of PLAINTIFF's safety complaints; (b) that YOU were motivated by animus toward PLAINTIFF for making those complaints; (c) that YOUR stated reason for termination was pretextual; (d) that YOU violated Labor Code section 6310 by retaliating against PLAINTIFF; and (e) that YOU acted with malice, oppression, or fraud.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 9:
Admit that the document identified as Bates No. GSC-000145 through GSC-000148, which is PLAINTIFF's employee performance review dated January 15, 2025, is authentic.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 10:
Admit that PLAINTIFF's performance review dated January 15, 2025 rated his overall performance as "Exceeds Expectations."

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 11:
Admit that YOU have a written policy prohibiting retaliation against employees who report workplace safety concerns.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 12:
Admit that Frank Delgado, YOUR Director of Operations, was the sole decision-maker regarding PLAINTIFF's termination.

I hereby certify service on all parties.
