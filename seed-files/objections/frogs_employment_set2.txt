PROPOUNDING PARTY: Plaintiff James Thompson
RESPONDING PARTY: Defendant Pacific Coast Bancorp
SET NUMBER: 1

FORM INTERROGATORIES — EMPLOYMENT LAW

The following Form Interrogatories are propounded pursuant to CCP section 2030.010 et seq. and Judicial Council Form DISC-002 (Employment Law). Defendant is requested to answer each interrogatory under oath.

INTERROGATORY NO. 200.1:
State the name, ADDRESS, and telephone number of each PERSON who was involved in any of the acts or omissions of which PLAINTIFF complains in this action. For each PERSON identified, describe the nature of that PERSON's involvement.

INTERROGATORY NO. 200.2:
State the dates on which PLAINTIFF was employed by YOU, each job title or position held by PLAINTIFF, and each location at which PLAINTIFF worked during employment with YOU.

INTERROGATORY NO. 200.3:
Identify all supervisors who had authority over PLAINTIFF during PLAINTIFF's employment with YOU, including the dates of each supervisory relationship and the title and position held by each supervisor.

INTERROGATORY NO. 200.4:
State all reasons for the adverse employment action(s) of which PLAINTIFF complains in this action. For each reason: (a) identify all PERSONS involved in making the decision; (b) state the date on which the decision was made; (c) identify all DOCUMENTS relating to the decision.

INTERROGATORY NO. 200.5:
Were any comparators — employees in the same or similar position as PLAINTIFF — treated differently from PLAINTIFF with respect to the employment actions of which PLAINTIFF complains? If so, for each comparator: (a) state the comparator's name; (b) identify the comparator's position and job duties; (c) describe how the comparator was treated differently; (d) state the reason for the different treatment.

INTERROGATORY NO. 201.1:
State all facts supporting any denial of PLAINTIFF's allegations that PLAINTIFF was subjected to discrimination based on a protected characteristic.

INTERROGATORY NO. 201.2:
Identify each PERSON who YOU contend had a legitimate, non-discriminatory reason for the actions about which PLAINTIFF complains and, for each such PERSON, state the legitimate, non-discriminatory reason.

INTERROGATORY NO. 202.1:
State all facts supporting any contention by YOU that PLAINTIFF's work performance was unsatisfactory.

INTERROGATORY NO. 202.2:
If YOU contend PLAINTIFF engaged in misconduct, describe with specificity each act of misconduct, including dates, witnesses, and any documents relating to each act.

INTERROGATORY NO. 203.1:
Did YOU investigate PLAINTIFF's complaints of harassment? If so: (a) identify the person(s) who conducted the investigation; (b) state the dates of the investigation; (c) identify each witness interviewed; (d) state the conclusions reached; (e) identify all corrective action taken, if any.

INTERROGATORY NO. 204.1:
At the time of the adverse employment action, did PLAINTIFF meet the minimum qualifications for the position held? If YOU contend PLAINTIFF did not meet the minimum qualifications, state all facts supporting that contention.

INTERROGATORY NO. 207.1:
State all facts upon which YOU base any affirmative defense to PLAINTIFF's claims, including the names of all PERSONS with knowledge of such facts and the identity of all supporting DOCUMENTS.

INTERROGATORY NO. 207.2:
If YOU contend that PLAINTIFF failed to exhaust administrative remedies, state all facts supporting that contention.

INTERROGATORY NO. 209.1:
If YOU contend that PLAINTIFF failed to mitigate damages, state: (a) the specific steps PLAINTIFF should have taken; (b) the positions or employment opportunities available to PLAINTIFF; (c) the amount by which YOU contend damages should be reduced.

INTERROGATORY NO. 210.1:
Have YOU entered into any agreement or understanding with any PERSON concerning the events at issue in this litigation, including any agreement to indemnify, hold harmless, or cooperate? If so, identify each such agreement, the parties, and the subject matter.
