PROPOUNDING PARTY: Plaintiff David Chen
RESPONDING PARTY: Defendant Pacific Logistics Corp.
SET NUMBER: 1

SPECIAL INTERROGATORIES

DEFINITIONS

"YOU" or "YOUR" means Defendant Pacific Logistics Corp. "PLAINTIFF" means Plaintiff David Chen. "DOCUMENT" means any writing as defined in Evidence Code Section 250. "CLASS PERIOD" means January 1, 2021 through October 31, 2025. "SIMILARLY SITUATED EMPLOYEES" means all non-exempt warehouse workers employed by YOU at YOUR Riverside, California facility.

INSTRUCTIONS

Answer each interrogatory separately and fully in writing under oath. Where an interrogatory asks for identification of DOCUMENTS, describe each DOCUMENT sufficiently for a request for production.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 1:
State the total number of hours worked by PLAINTIFF for each pay period during the CLASS PERIOD, including regular hours, overtime hours, and double-time hours.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 2:
For each pay period during the CLASS PERIOD, state whether PLAINTIFF was provided a thirty-minute uninterrupted meal period before the end of his fifth hour of work, and if not, state whether PLAINTIFF was compensated one additional hour of pay at his regular rate for each missed or interrupted meal period.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 3:
Describe in detail YOUR timekeeping system and all policies and practices relating to recording employee work time, including but not limited to time clocks, badge readers, manual timesheets, electronic timekeeping software, rounding policies, and any automatic deductions for meal periods.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 4:
State whether YOU have ever rounded PLAINTIFF's or any SIMILARLY SITUATED EMPLOYEE's clock-in or clock-out times, and if so, describe YOUR rounding policy and state whether such rounding has, in the aggregate over any period, resulted in employees being compensated for less time than actually worked.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 5:
State all facts supporting YOUR classification of PLAINTIFF as a non-exempt employee, including the primary duties performed by PLAINTIFF and the percentage of time spent on each duty.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 6:
Identify all lawsuits, administrative complaints, DLSE claims, PAGA notices, class action complaints, and any other legal proceedings filed against YOU by current or former employees alleging wage and hour violations, meal period violations, rest break violations, overtime violations, minimum wage violations, or any other Labor Code violations, at any time, in any jurisdiction.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 7:
State YOUR policy for authorizing and recording overtime work, and identify all instances during the CLASS PERIOD where PLAINTIFF worked in excess of eight hours in a day or forty hours in a week.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 8:
For each wage statement issued to PLAINTIFF during the CLASS PERIOD, state whether the wage statement contained all information required by Labor Code section 226(a), including: (a) gross wages earned; (b) total hours worked; (c) piece-rate units earned and applicable piece rate; (d) all deductions; (e) net wages earned; (f) inclusive dates of the period for which the employee is paid; (g) name and address of the legal entity that is the employer; (h) all applicable hourly rates and corresponding hours worked at each rate; (i) the employee's social security number or employee identification number.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 9:
State the amount of any and all waiting time penalties owed to PLAINTIFF pursuant to Labor Code section 203, including the daily rate and the number of days such penalties have accrued.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 10:
State whether YOU required or permitted PLAINTIFF or any SIMILARLY SITUATED EMPLOYEES to perform work off the clock, before clocking in, after clocking out, or during unpaid meal periods, and if so, describe such work and the approximate time spent.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 11:
State YOUR rest period policy during the CLASS PERIOD and whether PLAINTIFF was provided a net ten-minute paid rest period for every four hours worked or major fraction thereof.

SPECIAL INTERROGATORY NO. 12:
Describe in detail the training YOU provided to PLAINTIFF's supervisors and managers regarding wage and hour compliance, meal and rest break requirements, overtime calculation, and timekeeping accuracy.
