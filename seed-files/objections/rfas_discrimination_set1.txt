PROPOUNDING PARTY: Plaintiff Karen Williams
RESPONDING PARTY: Defendant Sunrise Senior Living Inc.
SET NUMBER: 1

REQUESTS FOR ADMISSION

DEFINITIONS

"YOU" or "YOUR" refers to Defendant Sunrise Senior Living Inc. "PLAINTIFF" refers to Karen Williams. "PROTECTED CHARACTERISTIC" refers to PLAINTIFF's race (African American) and gender (female).

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 1:
Admit that PLAINTIFF was employed by YOU as a Registered Nurse from June 10, 2021 through November 3, 2025.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 2:
Admit that PLAINTIFF received satisfactory or above-satisfactory performance evaluations during each year of her employment with YOU.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 3:
Admit that YOU terminated PLAINTIFF's employment on November 3, 2025.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 4:
Admit that PLAINTIFF complained to YOUR human resources department about racial discrimination on or about August 15, 2025.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 5:
Admit that no formal investigation was conducted into PLAINTIFF's complaint of racial discrimination.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 6:
Admit that PLAINTIFF's direct supervisor, Linda Marsh, made comments about PLAINTIFF's hair and appearance that referenced PLAINTIFF's race.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 7:
Admit that between January 1, 2023 and December 31, 2025, YOU terminated the employment of a disproportionate number of African American employees compared to employees of other races at YOUR Pasadena facility.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 8:
Admit that YOU failed to provide PLAINTIFF with any written performance improvement plan prior to her termination.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 9:
Admit or deny whether YOU believe racial discrimination is morally wrong and whether YOUR company has ever engaged in any form of racial bias, conscious or unconscious, in any employment decision at any time in YOUR company's history.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 10:
Admit that the document attached hereto as Exhibit A, which purports to be an email from Linda Marsh to HR Director James Powell dated October 28, 2025, is a genuine and authentic business record of YOUR company.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 11:
Admit that YOU did not provide anti-discrimination training to supervisors at YOUR Pasadena facility during calendar year 2025.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 12:
Admit that after PLAINTIFF's termination, YOU hired a non-African American employee to fill PLAINTIFF's position.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 13:
Admit that PLAINTIFF was qualified to perform the essential functions of the Registered Nurse position she held at the time of her termination and that she held all required licenses and certifications.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 14:
Admit that the stated reason for PLAINTIFF's termination — "restructuring" — was pretextual and that the true reason for PLAINTIFF's termination was her race, her gender, and/or her complaints of discrimination.

REQUEST FOR ADMISSION NO. 15:
Admit that PLAINTIFF filed a complaint with the California Civil Rights Department (CRD) on or about November 20, 2025, alleging racial discrimination and retaliation.
