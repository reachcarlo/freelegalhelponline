## Case Background

Roberto Mendez worked as a warehouse supervisor for Golden State Logistics for 3 years. He was classified as exempt but performed primarily non-exempt duties (loading, sorting, driving). He regularly worked 50-60 hours/week with no overtime pay, was required to work through lunch, and was not provided 10-minute rest breaks. He was terminated after raising pay concerns with management. James Patterson is the owner/operator who directly controlled pay practices.
