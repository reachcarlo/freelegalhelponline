## Case Background

Jennifer Nguyen was a VP of Compliance at Pacific Financial Group. She reported sexual harassment by the CFO to both HR and the company's board. Two weeks after her internal complaint, she was demoted to a non-client-facing role with a 30% pay cut. She then filed a complaint with CRD. One month later, she was terminated, ostensibly for "reorganization." She has been diagnosed with anxiety and depression as a result.
