## Case Background (for context)

She was terminated after filing an internal complaint about age and gender discrimination when she was passed over for promotion in favor of a younger male colleague with less experience. Her performance reviews were consistently "Exceeds Expectations" until the quarter after her complaint, when she was placed on a PIP.
