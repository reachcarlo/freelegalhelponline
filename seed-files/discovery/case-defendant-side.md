## Case Background

Kevin Brooks, a registered nurse at Sunrise Healthcare, claims he was terminated after requesting CFRA leave to care for his disabled child. Sunrise contends Brooks was terminated for documented patient care deficiencies over a 12-month period, including three written warnings. The defense position is that the termination was based on legitimate, non-discriminatory reasons and the timing was coincidental. Defense counsel is propounding discovery to support the performance-based termination.
