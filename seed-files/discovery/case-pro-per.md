## Case Background

Angela Martinez worked as a line cook for 2 years at a QuickServe location. She was paid $16/hour but regularly worked 45+ hours/week without overtime. She was required to clock out for lunch but continue working. After she asked the manager about overtime pay, her hours were reduced from 45 to 20 per week. She is representing herself and needs simple, clear discovery documents.
