# Agency Routing Guide — Seed Scenarios

URL: `/tools/agency-routing`

---

## Scenario 1: Unpaid Wages — Private Employer

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Issue Type** | Unpaid Wages/Wage Theft |
| **Government Employee** | No |

**Expected output:** DLSE (primary), small claims court (alternative), civil suit. Shows DLSE complaint process, Berman hearing info.

---

## Scenario 2: Discrimination — Private Employer

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Issue Type** | Discrimination (Race, Gender, Age, Disability, etc.) |
| **Government Employee** | No |

**Expected output:** CRD (prerequisite — must file first), EEOC (alternative federal route), court (after right-to-sue). Demos the administrative exhaustion requirement.
